### eWAR Fellowship Overview

- European venture builder, “Europe’s answer to Y Combinator”
- Extremely selective (10% acceptance rate in 2023, even more competitive now)
- Two pathways:
  - Ideation: Early stage, no co-founder yet
  - Traction: Established team with product development
- Looking for “outlier founders” who can build and sell
  - Example: Youngest ML researcher at Cambridge, joined Stephen Hawking’s group, built JP Morgan’s internal ChatGPT, got A16Z investment at 16
  - Example: National fencing champion doing AI masters at ETH/TUM
- Only CEO can apply to program
